Welcome!


This is the more personal side of my website, showcasing the book and codes notes I've created, a calendar consisting of my activity log for previous days, stock history of my trades, and my schedules.

The notes consists coding notes and book notes. I've created my coding notes when learning new languages or frameworks, either through co-op or learning on my own. However, I am most proud of my book notes. Being a voracious book reader and audiobook listening, I've highlighted various parts of books I've read which I find either helpful or relevant to me, wrote them down manually by hand in a notepad and typed them up for anyone to view.

The calendar consists of a log of my activities. After realizing how much time I'm wasting without being the least bit aware during my 2A school term, I decided to start creating a log of all my activities. In addition, I'm a big fan of regulating my personal finances so I've also included whatever expenses I've encurred during the day.

The stock history consists of all my trades I've done since starting investing in the summer of 2018. It consists of the stocks I buy, quantity, price, and the profit or loss I made after each sell. I am by no means a good investor, much less someone to follow.

The schedules consist of the schedules I've followed since January 2019. I developed my schedules to give myself a more clear path of what to do, when, after failing to keep up good habits in Fall 2018.

This website is by no means done. I'm looking forward on working towards more and different sections. Some things I have in mind is a personal blog and a section for side projects. I might add more styling, but I honestly prefer to keep things simple.
